Finding


2 out of 4 clusters have significantly more compact, bulge-dominated galaxies than field galaxies.
An established morphology-density relationship!

The other two clusters are intriguing: separated by 73 Mpc in 3D space, there is a high chance of contamination from one cluster to another; or from possible filamentary galaxies.
A deeper HST study of the J1432.3-4 region is crucial!
